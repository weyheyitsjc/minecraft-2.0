Controls
  • s: Swap between main and rotating camera
  • Space: Turn on/off the flashlight.
Main camera control:
  • Forward/Backward arrow: Move forward/backward.
  • Right/Left arrow: Move left/right.
  • Z/z: Roll counterclockwise/clockwise. This is a rotation about the z axis.
  • X/x: Look up/down. This is a rotation about the u axis.
  • C/c: Turn left/right. This is a rotation about the v axis.
