Forward/Backward arrow: Move forward/backward.
Right/Left arrow: Move left/right.
Z/z: Roll counterclockwise/clockwise in the uv plane. This is a rotation about the z axis.
X/x: Pitch up/down in the vn plane. This is a rotation about the u axis.
C/c: Yaw counterclockwsise/clockwise in the un plane. This is a rotation about the v axis.
Space: Turn on/off the flashlight.
